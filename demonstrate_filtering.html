Thumbnail Filtering Demonstration

Showing how videos without proper thumbnails are filtered out

How It Works

The system automatically filters out videos that don't have proper thumbnail images. This includes:

  • Videos with empty or missing thumbnail URLs
  • Videos with placeholder images (placehold.co, etc.)
  • Videos with thumbnails containing text like "Image Not Found" or "No Image"
  • Videos with invalid URL formats

Only videos with genuine thumbnail images are displayed on the website.
